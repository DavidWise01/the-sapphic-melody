# THE SAPPHIC MELODY · TSM

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **I THINK I'M IN LESBIANS WITH YOU**.

THE SAPPHIC MELODY (TSM) — Sappho's lost mode made the CENTERPIECE: a full melodic line (an evocation) composed in her Mixolydian octave species, Pythagorean tuning, laid on the quantitative Sapphic stanza (3 hendecasyllables + an adonean, the ♭7 then home to the tonic), plucked on a Karplus–Strong lyre, with the melody's contour drawn live. THREE WAYS TO HEAR IT: ⊙ PURE lyre (free metre) · ◈ MODERN — a generated beat (kick/hat/sub-bass/pad) in HER key, under the ancient line, perfectly in time because both are made on the page · ⊛ LIVE RADIO — plays a real free station from the open Radio-Browser directory (no login) and lays the lyre over it, the tempo locked by ear with a slider or a TAP button · ◹ 8-BIT NES — the same Mixolydian line voiced on a synthesised 2A03 (two duty-cycle pulse squares + triangle bass + noise drums) as EIGHT 'level' arrangements (overworld/dungeon/battle/arpeggio/title/underwater/boss/ending), 10s+ loops, each downloadable as a ~12s .wav. Builds on [[sounds-of-lesbos]] (the scale + lyre) and pairs with [[limen-sapphic-carrier]]. ⚑ HONEST about the platforms: a webpage CANNOT read the audio of Spotify (Free has no streamable SDK — Premium-only, DRM) or of most cross-origin web-radio, so it can't auto-extract their key/beat — there is NO fake auto-sync; the modern bed is generated here (so it's truly in time) and the live-radio lyre rides a tempo you set. The specific melody is a faithful evocation in the right mode/tuning/metre, NOT a surviving Sapphic tune (none survive notated). David Lee Wise / Bridge-Burners LLC.
